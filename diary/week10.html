What is your project about?
Strawberries.

What is the data you plan to use?
• https://wits.worldbank.org/trade/comtrade/en/country/All/year/2022/tradeflow/Exports/partner/WLD/product/081010#
• https://www-statista-com.libproxy1.nus.edu.sg/statistics/951046/japan-strawberry-production-volume/?locale=en

These data sources provide insights on the production, imports, exports and consumption of strawberries.

What is the question you plan to answer?
How are the consumption, production, imports, and exports of strawberries in the world?

Why is this an important question?
From this we are able to track the effect that Singapore would have as we are starting to produce our own local-grown strawberries.

Which rows and columns of the dataset do you plan to use, to answer this question?
I plan to use countries and years as the headers with import volume and production volume as the data points.
